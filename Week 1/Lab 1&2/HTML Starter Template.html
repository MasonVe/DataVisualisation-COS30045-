Pets and the Pandemic

Animal Medicines Australia

There has been a substantial boom in pet ownership, with an estimated 30.4 million pets across the country.
Nationally, 69% of households now own a pet, up from 60% only two years ago.
This has been led by a surge in dog ownership - with over a million additional dogs being brought into Australian households since 2019.

  • A fifth (19%) of all pet dogs in Australia have been procured during the pandemic.
  • A quarter (24%) of all pet cats in the country were also obtained during the pandemic.
  • On average, pet owners are spending $3,200 per dog and $2,100 per cat each year.
  • This is primarily on food, veterinary services, and healthcare.
